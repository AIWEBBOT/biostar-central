Groups

Groups are subsections of the site dedicated to a particular topic. Some groups are public (visible to everyone) others may be private (visible to subscribed users only). To gain membership to a private group the moderators of that group will need to add members to their group.

Users with 500 reputation points or higher may create a group. Only a limited number of groups may be created by each user. Site admins may also create groups both public and private.
